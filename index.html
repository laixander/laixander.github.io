About

Hi! I'm Laixander. I'm a Web Designer based in the Philippines. I specialize in web mainly designing clean, accessible and intuitive websites using the latest web standards and technology.

Adobe Creative Suite and VSCode are some of the tools I use for creating graphics and websites, making these live and functional through HTML, CSS and Javascript.

When I'm not working, I spend most of my time reading, listening to music, riding a bike, and taking pictures. But sometimes I just sit and relax, drink a lot of coffee and learn something new.
